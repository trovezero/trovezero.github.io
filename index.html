Q&A

Learn more about the different aspects of Interest Management.

How does it work?

The mechanism maintains interest rates by targeting 10-40% of total debt as redeemable before us. When adjustments are needed, it aims for 25% - the midpoint of our target range.

What are the key parameters?

The system uses three main parameters: a minimum threshold of 10%, a maximum threshold of 40%, and a 3-day grace period for adjustments above the maximum threshold.

Why use our system?

Our system provides automated interest rate management that helps maintain stability while minimizing the frequency of rate changes. This results in more predictable borrowing costs. Our team has many years of experience in DeFi.

Is it secure?

The system has undergone thorough testing and auditing. The smart contracts are open-source and verified on Etherscan. However, as with any DeFi protocol, users should understand the risks involved.
